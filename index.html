How it works
• Pick your name in Predict, fill in scores and tap Lock In
• Locked predictions can't be changed
• New rounds appear automatically as the tournament progresses
• Scores sync live from the football database
• The leaderboard updates as games finish
• Knockout games decided on penalties: the score that counts is extra-time goals + penalty shootout goals added together per team (e.g. 2–2 after extra time, won 4–3 on pens → counts as 6–5). Guess that combined total exactly for 3 pts, just the winning team for 2 pts, or one team's number exactly for 1 pt.
